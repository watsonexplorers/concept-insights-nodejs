Concept Insights

Concept Insights allows you to discover content which may or may not be found using a traditional keyword search.
Resources:
  • API Overview
  • Documentation
  • Fork and Deploy on Bluemix
  • Fork on Github

    • Try the service

    This service uses a pre-existing graph of concepts (based on Wikipedia) to surface the concepts in your input. It will use these concepts to recommend relevant content from the corpus you ingest. For this demo, discover TED talks using topics, words, or concepts. Or, paste in some text and we'll identify the concepts for you.

    Output

    TED talks may have explicit concept matches from your input. However, they also have implicit matches, with a single related concept or a collection of concepts. You may be surprised at what you would miss using a traditional keyword search.
