Concept Insights DEMO by Watson Explorers of Fort Collins

Concept Insights allows you to discover content which may or may not be found using a traditional keyword search.

Try the service

This service uses a pre-existing graph (a fancy database) of concepts (based on English Wikipedia) to surface (to link or match) the concepts in your input. It will use these concepts to recommend relevant content from the corpus we ingested (the documents we uploaded to Watson). For this demo, we've uploaded a few documents related to Health and Fitness. The output comes from those documents, Wikipedia, and the concepts you input below.
